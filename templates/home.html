Strengths

Courtesy of the CliftonStrengths assessment tool developed by The Gallup Organization. This tool helps individuals discover their top five strengths and can help organizations improve company culture and performance.

Futuristic

As a first-gen Latina grad, I have always considered myself a "go-getter." Setting goals is a must because it allows me to get more done by creating a clear vision for the future.

Restorative

I'm a true problem-solver at heart. If I'm unfamilar with a concept, I tackle it until acquiring the necessary skills that will allow me to produce obtainable results.

Input

I have always had and always will have a craving to know more. As someone from a low-income background, I was always taught that knowledge is power. It controls the access to opportunity and advancement.

Analytical

Thanks to my background in biochemistry, I have a thing for finding the right data to back up a cause and taking into account all of the possible reasons for its occurence. I've translated this to my career as a sofware engineer.

Significance

Industrious, diligent, and persistent are words that I would use to describe myself. I am no stranger to hard work and am willing to persist until the very end, but prefer to work where my skills are valued.

Recent Projects

A peak of some things I've done or am currently working on!

  • Choro-Tracker

    Choro-Tracker is a full stack interactive tracker for global COVID-19 data. The app allows a user to interact with a global COVID-19 dataset through use of a Chart.js choropleth map and line graph.

  • Latina Dev

    Latina Dev is a free, open source website that will provide a number of different free resources to its subscribers such as tools for learning software development, a place for other Latina developers to connect and network, and monthly newsletters detailing the addition of new content and possible future events.

  • Trio Joblist

    Freelance full stack web development project tackling the automation of repetitive tasks and revamping of a website for an out-of-state client who reached out to me through LinkedIn!
